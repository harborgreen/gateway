about Harbor Green Group

About Our Group

Harbor Green Resources Development Holding, Inc. is a registered corporation of California, USA

With over 1,000 business associates, two decades of industry experience, 10 comapnies across Utah, Arizona, Nevada, and California, we are an industry leader in material resources

Our Mission

Our vision is to optimize the reuse of recovered products by rebuilding them into viable products in order to decrease the strain on our world's natural resource usage.

Whether in the fiber markets of recovered paper and plastic, or the recycling markets for ferrours and non-ferrous metals, our driving vision is to create a better future for us all.

Expert Knowledge

With over 1,000 employees over 10 companies, we have over 20 years of on-the-job experience in this industry.

We are a proud leading member of the Institute of Scrap Recycling Industry, and we strive to fulfill the full responsibilities of our role as industry leader.

Market Dominance

Our market presence allows us to leverage the full force of international trade.

Combined with our expert and diverse personnel, we are able to dominate business not only in the United States, but also China, India, South America, Korea, Vietnam, and other South East Asia countries.
